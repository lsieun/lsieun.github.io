Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science
dedicated to creating systems capable of performing tasks
that typically require human intelligence,
such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, and decision-making.
It powers technologies like chatbots, self-driving cars,
and recommendation engines by analyzing data for patterns.
