```text
There's nothing in this world
that can trouble you as much as your own thoughts.

The hardest things to deal with are often the thoughts we keep running through our heads.
We get lost in our own worries, making everything feels much bigger than it really is.
But when we start to take control of our minds, we realize how much peace we can actually find.
```
